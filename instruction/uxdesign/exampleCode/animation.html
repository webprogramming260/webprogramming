Functional Animation Lab

Animation in web design should never be arbitrary. Motion should explain logic, direct attention, or provide feedback.

Effective Motion = Easing + Intentional Duration + Clear Feedback

Linear motion feels robotic because real objects accelerate and decelerate. Use easing to make interactions feel natural and less jarring.

Most interface transitions feel best in the 200-400ms range. Too slow can feel sluggish, too fast can be missed.

  • Easing mimics physics and improves perceived quality.
  • Timing controls comprehension and task speed.
  • Pull-to-refresh teaches behavior through motion sequence.
